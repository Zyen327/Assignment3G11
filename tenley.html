Tenley's Story

Tenley is my newest furry family member, adopted at about a year old—she's 4 now, and time flies! After losing Maxwell, I began volunteering at a local shelter. Every morning, this affectionate, curious cat greeted me, and I knew she was the one. Tenley settled into my home instantly and is one of the sweetest cats I’ve known. She adores cuddles, endless pets, and has boundless energy. Her favorite quirk? Dragging her fishing rod around at 3 AM, meowing for attention. She and Mona, my other cat, aren’t the best of friends—Tenley’s outgoing, high-energy vibe doesn’t mesh with Mona’s anxious nature. Still, they’ve learned to peacefully coexist, and that’s good enough for me!
